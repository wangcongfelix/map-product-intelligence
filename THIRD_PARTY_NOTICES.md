This project is being redesigned as a map product intelligence workspace. Its core business will focus on importing user comments, release notes, and competitor materials; reviewing AI-assisted product signals; maintaining a confirmed signal table; and preparing analysis reports. These V1 capabilities are not implemented in Stage 0.
